Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the process take?

Most custom painted shoes take 2-3 weeks to complete from the date of order confirmation. Complex designs may take up to 4 weeks. You'll receive progress updates throughout the process.

Are the custom painted designs waterproof?

Yes! All of our designs are sealed with a water-resistant and flexible finisher that protects the artwork while allowing the shoes to flex naturally. However, we recommend avoiding heavy rain or puddles to maximize the lifespan of your custom shoes.

Can I wash my custom shoes?

We recommend spot cleaning only. Machine washing may damage the artwork.

Can I request revisions to the design?

We provide 2 rounds of digital revisions before beginning the painting process. You can request reasonable adjustments at this stage. Once painting begins, major changes may incur additional fees.
